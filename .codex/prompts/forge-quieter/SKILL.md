# /forge-quieter — Soften Design

Reduce visual intensity while staying within your system:

1. Headlines: move down 1 type scale step
2. Accent: shift from 600 to 500 or 400
3. Shadows: reduce by 1 elevation level
4. Reduce letter-spacing on headings
5. Increase whitespace for calm

All changes stay within your system's defined range.
